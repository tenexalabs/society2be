Path to better living

Guidance posts for society problems

How to report problems clearly

Mention the exact location, photo evidence if available, safety risk, and whether the issue affects one flat, one tower, or the whole society.

How residents can help committees

Use one official channel, avoid duplicate complaints, give realistic response time, and volunteer when a topic needs resident support.

How to build trust

Publish status, explain delays, respect staff, close the loop after work is done, and keep decisions visible.
